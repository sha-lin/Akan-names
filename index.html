AKAN NAMES.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Akan names are given to children according to the day of the week they were born. Akan names are also based on the gender of the baby born as one cannot get a female Akan name if he or she is male. These Akan names play a big role in ones life
Using this website, you will be able to know not only your Akan name but also the day of the week you were born.
